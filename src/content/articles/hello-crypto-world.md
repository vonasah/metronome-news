Welcome to **Metronome News** — a fresh voice in crypto and Web3 journalism.

This is our first article, and it marks the start of something we're genuinely excited about. The crypto news landscape is noisy, full of shilling, and often disconnected from the technology it's supposed to cover. We want to do it differently.

## What we cover

Our focus is on eight core areas:

- **Bitcoin** — ETF flows, on-chain data, macro narratives
- **Ethereum** — upgrades, L2 ecosystem, DeFi on mainnet
- **Altcoins** — everything beyond BTC and ETH that matters
- **DeFi** — protocols, yield, risks, and the never-ending innovation cycle
- **NFTs** — past the hype, into real use cases
- **Regulation** — what lawmakers do and how it affects you
- **Market Analysis** — data over opinion
- **Web3** — the broader decentralized stack

## How we're different

Three principles guide us:

1. **No shilling.** We don't take payment to cover projects.
2. **Sources over speculation.** Every claim links to its origin.
3. **Respect your time.** Short when possible, long only when needed.

## What's next

Over the coming weeks you'll see our first deep dives — starting with a piece on Bitcoin ETF flows in Q2 2026, and a closer look at the latest Ethereum L2 landscape.

If you find what we publish useful, the best thing you can do is share it with someone who also reads too much crypto news. We'll take it from there.

Welcome aboard. 🎵
